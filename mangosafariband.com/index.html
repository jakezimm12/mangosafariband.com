About Us

Mango Safari is a indie rock band formed in New Hope, Pennsylvania in 2018. The band consists of Henry Cabelus, Grant Cheung, Derek Donohoe, Ethan Datz, and Ryan Keating. Mango Safari takes a DIY approach to making their music and records and mixes all their music at Mango Safari HQ, a scenic 1800s home reconfigured into a recording studio where the band records both digital and analog . The result is a unique sound with hints of vintage lo-fi elements mixed with spacey sonic journeys that will leave you wanting to put the song on repeat. Check out the bands Instagram @mango_safari to hear new music and buy tickets to their next show!
